## Overview

This repository contains materials, notes, activities, and supporting resources for an NSF Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) workshop on agentic AI for research, hosted at the University of North Texas.

The workshop focuses on how agentic AI systems can support research workflows, including literature exploration, planning, coding, data analysis, documentation, and critical evaluation of AI-assisted outputs.

## Workshop Goals

- Introduce participants to agentic AI concepts and research use cases.
- Practice using AI agents for structured research tasks.
- Build habits for evaluating, verifying, and documenting AI-assisted work.
- Support participants in building research workflows they can inspect, reproduce, and explain.
